--- Slide 8 ---
Gammaproteobacteria
Largest and most diverse, many human pathogens
Order Pseudomonadales
Common in soils, produce antibiotics & may protect plants
Opportunistic pathogens
Metabolically diverse, can grow on unusual nutrients (even in disinfectants)
P. aeruginosa (cystic fibross, burn patients): very antibiotic resistant
Acinetobacter baumanii: extremely antibiotic resistant, hospital infections
Order Enterobacteriales
Include coliforms (E.coli & others)
Order Vibrionales
Found in waters, often coastal
Vibrio cholerae (cholera), parahemolyticus, vulnificus (flesh eating)
Others: Haemophilus influenza (pneumonia), Francisella (rabbit fever), Legionella
